Behind the Bar

Snickers is a chocolate bar made by Mars, Incorporated. It is nougat topped with caramel and peanuts, coated in milk chocolate. It was introduced in 1930. It has had sales of over $2 Billion around the world.The very first SNICKERS Bar, allegedly named after a horse, was manufactured in Chicago and sold for five cents.
